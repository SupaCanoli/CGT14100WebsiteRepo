This is not good. This is really, really, not good. I've been communicating with Angela a lot more lately as she's been trying to figure out more about why the company wants full rights to everything that we make throughout the company, maybe to make some point that could be held up to make a new standard, but instead, things have become much more serious. It appears that all of our innovations and data have been used by the higher-ups with assistance of a different team to make versions of our new tech that is specialized for use in violent scenarios. Essentially, they're turning our ideas and designs into a sandbox for war weapons. With the being that I've become throughout this process, and the things I'm capable of, including the things I originally planned on documenting here, I believe are enough to take over an entire country in the wrong hands. If there's anything I've wanted to do less now, it's hand off information about this invention, especially if it means I'd have a hand in possibly ending an entire country.

Regardless, I should probably mention this newfound capability I've been training, as I just explained it to Angela before I had another bout regarding it. It appears that because the technological portion of my biology is so miniscule, it seems to be able to go into a quantum superposition, swapping data with what could theoretically be other versions of myself from other universes! It's certainly a far fetch, but I haven't been proven wrong so far considering the occasional memory that pops up in the technological sector making up my brain. At least it doesn't seem to be attempting to overwrite the original biological portions of my brain at all. After all, I created this thing with the intention of it not being able to modify actual components about the human brain. As a temporary name, I'll call this phenomenon "Quantum Memory Thrashing", to represent a memory swap, similar to how computers can swap memory into other places in the system when overloading or being unstable. The hope I have though is to be able to train this into something I can use intentionally, to search for something specific.

Now that I know that this is possible, I really want to know how feasible it is to pull information from other versions of myself to try and figure out the best way to get out of the situation I've been in. Maybe, just maybe... a full universe switch wouldn't be too bad to get away from this company? Maybe there's a universe out there that does a better job of taking care of their employees, or lets them try their ideas as their ideas instead of stealing it for a damn war weapon. I'll keep working on it and updating as I go in a new log for this universe idea.

~Keeping sincerely in mind, Supa.
