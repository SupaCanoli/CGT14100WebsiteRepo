General Overview of Supa

Supa is a character that began its conceptualization around 2021 to 2022, with very low amounts of actual characterization or building being given at that time. Since then, he has changed enough to constitute his own logging system for how he became who he is in his whole story! This website is here to dedicate "files" pulled from his laboratory work, that talk about his experiment to make a human that was fully blended to coexist with technology, for the sake of improving the general quality of life of the general public. Doing this led to him to be so confident in his work, that he tested it on himself, which led to many different following events, including an entire universe switch, changing his life's path entirely! These events are better described by him throughout his following logs as he explains his mistakes and successes. Enjoy!
