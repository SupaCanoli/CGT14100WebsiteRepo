This new universe idea is quite daunting, but there isn't really much other choice that I have if I want to escape the chance of this power falling into the wrong hands anymore. I have to do this. On the bright side, there seems to be a good portion of time still before I am required to show anything to the company, so I've been taking my time trying to calm myself down by relearning an old skill I had when I was still in college, bartending! Making mocktails was certainly fun, so in my downtime I've been trying to keep that up. To the left is a loop I got while I mixed something nice in a simulative environment :).

In other news, Angela came to the door again today to talk. She seemed a bit put off, for some reason, but something started happening with my test run in the background, so I had to leave earlier than expected. I do hope she's doing okay though. I guess her now knowing that I'm planning on leaving with an invention she knows so little about is most certainly something that puts me in a bad light, but I can only hope she understands.

I'll keep on trying, but I think that this idea might actually work out! I've almost finished understanding and building enough of this one-way that it might just be feasible within the next couple months to do it fully safely. Right now, it seems like it can't do more than a one-way gate, meaning even if I end up in the right place, there's no way for me to do any traveling back, or to other universes. I also haven't been able to test whether or not it affects my specific biology yet. These memories I keep pulling are in a somewhat out of order fashion. I've been able to make sense of it up until now, but I can't seem to ensure what kind of matter can go through without being modified on the other end. I'll keep testing for another hour or so before I try getting some rest, but I'm very happy with my progress up until now understanding all of this.
