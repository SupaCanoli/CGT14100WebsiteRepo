Something went wrong. It was never supposed to make the human body so... fluid. I think I know what caused this issue, but it's not like I can reverse this super easily right now. I've included some images to represent what I've become below, but it certainly hasn't been too difficult to hold my new form.

The biological composition of my new body is capable of shapeshifting into different forms quite easily. The shapeshifting begins after the technological section of merged cells, as mentioned in Log # 1, begins glowing. The glowing is expected, before fixing certain parts of the body as more of an indicator, but being able to shift the form of my entire body freely is most certainly not expected. The third image from left to right (or top to bottom, if my log system is still working correctly for differently sized screens?) is an artistic recreation of the glowing effect that is given when the body begins shifting into a new form, or fixing any issues that may have been in the body at that time. It seems that the nanotechnology will form a connected design around the body before stopping the glowing effect, to do what seems to be a confirmation of a successful shift or fix. I'll need to do more research to confirm this theory later, but I am pretty confident in my understanding of how this works so far.

I will say that the shapeshifting itself is most certainly a trainable skill, but I definitely have a head-start considering how much I already know about this biological composition. Even so, I didn't necessarily get to choose exactly how I look as of now, but I'm not against keeping this similar structure as a baseline to refer back to when doing more testing in the future. As much as I would've wanted my original body, it doesn't seem like that will be possible considering the new pigment my skin has taken on from this operation.

There don't seem to be any other side effects that I should be worried about yet, though I will still see what kind of test runs I can do at the moment to combat negative effects happening in the future. I will say, however, that no matter which form I choose to take on, I seem to retain the exact same density althroughout my body. I will weigh significantly more the smaller my form is, and significantly less the larger the form is. The last image is one of the temporary forms that I was caught in on my camera as I attempted to shift into a more permanent form while learning how to control aforementioned shapeshifting. It's quite interesting to see how heavy I showed up as to my scale system while I was in that form. Apparently, I reached over 200 pounds while being at around the size of a foot tall, but I don't have a perfect understanding of how that scales, or even if it is proportional to other sizes. More research is needed at another time.
